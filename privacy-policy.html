Privacy Policy

How Ratnatraya Institute collects, uses and protects your information.

Last Updated: June 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Ratnatraya Institute ("we", "us", "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and how it is used when you visit our website or interact with us via WhatsApp, Telegram, email, or our inquiry forms.

By using our website, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

When you fill out our Admission/Enrollment form or Contact form, we may collect the following information:

  • Full Name — to address you correctly and personalize communication
  • Email Address — to respond to your queries and send updates
  • Phone / WhatsApp Number — to confirm enrollment and provide study material access
  • Education Level — to recommend the right course for you
  • Selected Exam — to understand your preparation goals
  • Message / Inquiry Details — whatever you choose to share with us

3. How We Use Your Information

The information you provide is used solely to:

  • Confirm your free enrollment and add you to our Telegram study groups
  • Respond to your inquiries via WhatsApp or Email within 24 hours
  • Send you relevant updates about courses, mock tests, and study material
  • Improve our courses based on student needs and feedback

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party for marketing purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

Our website uses the following third-party services, which may process your data according to their own privacy policies:

  • Formspree — processes form submissions (Admission & Contact forms) and delivers them to our email. See Formspree's Privacy Policy.
  • WhatsApp — when you click our WhatsApp button, you are redirected to WhatsApp's platform, governed by WhatsApp's own privacy policy.
  • Telegram — joining our Telegram channel/group is governed by Telegram's privacy policy.
  • Google Drive — test papers and mock tests are hosted on Google Drive, governed by Google's privacy policy.
  • Google Fonts & Particles.js — used for website design and visual effects, loaded from third-party CDNs.

5. Cookies & Analytics

We may use Google Analytics in the future to understand how visitors use our website (e.g., which pages are most visited). This helps us improve our content and courses. Google Analytics may use cookies to collect anonymous usage data.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal data
  • Withdraw consent for communication at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at ratnatrayainstitute@gmail.com.

8. Children's Privacy

Our courses are designed for competitive exam aspirants, generally above the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
