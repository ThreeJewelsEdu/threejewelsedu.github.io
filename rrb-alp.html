CBAT

CBAT Preparation

What is CBAT?

A psychometric aptitude test measuring traits essential for a Loco Pilot — attention, perception, and decision making.

Following Directions

Tests your ability to follow complex multi-step instructions accurately and quickly.

Depth Perception & Speed

Tests visual judgment of distance and speed — crucial for safe train operation.

Why Practice Matters

CBAT cannot be "studied" in the traditional sense — but regular practice with sample tests significantly improves scores.
