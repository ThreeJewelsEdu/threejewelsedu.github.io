Our Story

Born from a Simple Belief

Ratnatraya Institute was born from a simple yet powerful belief — that every aspirant deserves access to honest, ethical, and high-quality coaching without financial barriers.

Named after the three jewels of Jain philosophy — Right Knowledge (Samyak Jnana), Right Path (Samyak Charitra), and Right Vision (Samyak Darshan) — we built this institute on the foundation of truth, wisdom, and integrity.

We are a 100% online coaching institute dedicated to helping students crack Bank PO/Clerk, SSC CGL/CHSL, and Railway exams through structured learning, bilingual content, and ethical guidance.

Our name is not just a brand — it is our promise to every student who walks this path with us.

The Philosophy

The Meaning Behind Ratnatraya

Ratnatraya — रत्नत्रय — is the sacred concept from Jain philosophy referring to the Three Jewels that lead a soul toward liberation. These three jewels are not just our name — they are our promise to every student who joins us.

Jewel I 💎 सम्यक दर्शन

Samyak Darshan

Right Vision

See your goal clearly. Believe in yourself completely. Right faith is the foundation of every victory — without vision, knowledge has no direction.

For students: Believe you can crack your exam. Set your goal. Stay committed.
Jewel II 💎 सम्यक ज्ञान

Samyak Jnana

Right Knowledge

Study smart. Learn from the right sources. Right knowledge separates the truly prepared from the rest — quality over quantity, always.

For students: Study the right syllabus, from the right material, with the right guidance.
Jewel III 💎 सम्यक चरित्र

Samyak Charitra

Right Conduct

Stay disciplined. Be consistent. Never compromise on integrity. The right path leads to the right victory — no shortcuts, no unfair means.

For students: Be disciplined daily. Practice consistently. Never give up on ethics.
