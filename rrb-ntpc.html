Detailed Syllabus

Core

CBT 1 & CBT 2 Syllabus

General Awareness

Current Affairs, History, Geography, Polity, Economy, Science & Technology, Sports, Railways GK

Mathematics

Number System, Decimals, Fractions, LCM/HCF, Ratio, Percentage, Time & Work, Profit & Loss, Geometry, Trigonometry

General Intelligence & Reasoning

Analogies, Alphabetical Series, Coding-Decoding, Mathematical Operations, Venn Diagrams, Syllogism, Data Sufficiency

Note

Important Information

Posts Covered

Station Master, Junior Clerk cum Typist, Goods Guard, Senior Commercial cum Ticket Clerk, Traffic Assistant and more.

Skill Test

Typing test for clerical posts. Computer Based Aptitude Test (CBAT) for Station Master and Traffic Assistant posts.

Final Selection

Based on CBT 2 performance + skill test/CBAT (where applicable) + document verification + medical exam.
